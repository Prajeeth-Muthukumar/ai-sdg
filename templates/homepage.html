What is MaMaCare?

MaMaCare is a web-based machine learning model
designed to predict pregnancy risk levels
based on provided vitals. By analyzing key health vitals,
MaMaCare helps expectant mothers and
healthcare providers make informed decisions for a safer pregnancy journey.

Why MaMaCare

Pregnancy is a crucial phase where
early risk detection can save lives.
Many complications arise due to
undetected risks, and timely intervention is key.
MaMaCare bridges this gap
by using AI-driven predictions,
offering expectant mothers personalized
insights and proactive care.

How MaMaCare Works??

MaMaCare is powered by Machine Learning, specifically the RandomForestClassifier algorithm.
1.The model analyzes vital parameters
such as blood pressure, sugar levels,heart rate, and body temperature..
2.It classifies the pregnancy risk into Low, Medium, or High categories..
3.The system provides recommendations based on risk levels,
enabling early medical attention if needed..
